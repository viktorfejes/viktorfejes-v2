Viktor is the Creative Director of Gild Studios, which he founded to realise wonderful and captivating creations for the ad industry. He is a retoucher and 3d artist by trade, and an English philologist by education. Though, the latter has no bearing on his life except sometimes going "uhm, actually" when something vaguely related to linguistics comes up.

You might have seen him around on educational platforms and conferences, including CreativeLive, KelbyOne, or KTE as he believes sharing is vital for the advancement of this craft. His most useful tools can be found on his Gumroad and his free videos on YouTube. In 2019 he was named as one of Forbes' 30 under 30. He is not under thirty anymore.

Some things he likes, including but not limited to, and in no particular order: coding, cooking, mechanical keyboards, soulsborne games, mountains, challenges, and diving into new projects.

If you want to contact him you can join his Discord channel or send him an email. He lives in Los Angeles, CA.
