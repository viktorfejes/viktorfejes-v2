Viktor Fejes is a Digital Artist working in the advertising field.

Viktor is the Creative Director of Gild Studios, where he works to realise wonderful and captivating creations for the ad industry. He is a retoucher and 3d artist by trade, and an English philologist by education. Though, the latter has no bearing on his life except for knowing words like fricative.

You might have seen him around on educational websites, including CreativeLive, KelbyOne, or KTE as he likes to share his knowledge with anyone willing to learn. In 2019 he was named as one of Forbes' 30 under 30. In his spare time he likes to code, cook, and to tinker with mechanical keyboards. If you want to contact him you can get on his Discord channel or send him an email. He lives in Los Angeles, CA with his inimitable wife, Flora.
